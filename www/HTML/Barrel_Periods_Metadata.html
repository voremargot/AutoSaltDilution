Data Sources and Notes

All data in this table has been copied from the device magic form that is filled out during site visits.

PeriodID: This value is autogenerated when a new record is added.
SiteID: This corresponds to the primary key in the site description table and tells us what site the barrel fill occurred at.
Starting Date: The date when the barrel was refilled with new solution (YYYY-MM-DD).
Ending Date: The date when the next refill occurs (YYYY-MM-DD).
Solution at Start: The amount of salt solution in the reservoir when the barrel period began.
Solution at End: The amount of salt solution in the reservoir at the end of the barrel period.
Salt Added: The amount of salt that was added when the solution was being refilled.
Salt Remaining on Site: The amount of salt that is on site for future refills.
Notes: Notes taken in the field during reservoir refill.

Units of Data

Solution at Start --> L (rounded to the nearest 25L)
Solution at End --> L (rounded to the nearest 25L)
Salt Added --> kg
Salt Remaining on Site --> kg

Data Constraints

1. The Starting date must occur before the ending date.
2. The ending date of barrel period x will usually be the same as the starting date for period x+1.
3. When adding a new barrel fill event to this table, be sure to edit the ending date of the previous barrel fill from null to a valid date. This will ensure that there is only one active barrel fill period per site.
